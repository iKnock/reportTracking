What is this Temp?

Addis Essentials is a multiplatform (phone, tablet, desktop) application which provides it user with Geo-location based information while giving the capability of Rating, commenting and creating custom essentials list for its users.

Addis Essentials also provide users to easily check in to an essential and share it across social media. On the other hand other users can know how many users were checked in by gender. This might help users to easily identify the most popular or the hottest place in the city. It is also a platform to promote businesses effectively and efficiently.
